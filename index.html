Welcome to New Forward Care
A new and innovative service that provides personalised and holistic care for people with autism and mental health issues.

We believe that everyone deserves to live a fulfilling and meaningful life,
and we are here to support you on your journey.

At New Forward Care, we understand that each person is unique and has different needs and goals.
That is why we work with you on a one-on-one basis, to tailor our care to your preferences and aspirations.
We offer a range of services, such as:

  • Community-based support, to help you access social, educational and employment opportunities.
  • Advocacy and advice, to help you express your views and access your rights.
  • Peer support, to help you connect with others who share similar experiences.

Our team consists of qualified and experienced professionals, who have a passion for working with people with autism and mental health issues.
We also work closely with your family, carers and other agencies, to ensure that you receive coordinated and consistent care.

If you are interested in finding out more about our service, please CONTACT US today.
We would love to hear from you and discuss how we can help you achieve your goals and live the life you want.
